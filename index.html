Currently there are an estimated 2.7 million minor children with at least one parent behind bars, and at least 10 million have experienced parental incarceration at some point in their lives.There is currently a lack of support for children of incarcerated parents, which leaves room for cycles to begin and never end. We providing support to children of incarcerated parents through support groups and creative coping skill workshops.

Out of Bounds will assist in fostering and maintaining relationships between parents and their children through programming and special projects.
