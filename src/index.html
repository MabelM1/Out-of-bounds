Our Mission

Out of Bounds Support focuses on prevention by showing the children of incarcerated parents that Love and Life have No Bounds.

prev

next

Currently there are an estimated 2.7 million minor children with at least one parent behind bars, and at least 10 million children who have experienced parental incarceration at some point in their lives. There is currently a lack of support for children of incarcerated parents, which leaves room for cycles to begin and never end. Out of Bounds Support’s will assist children of incarcerated parents through support groups and creative coping skill workshops.

Out of Bounds Support will assist in fostering and maintaining relationships between parents and their children through programming and special projects. To see the work Out of Bounds Support has already done please visit the organization’s Instagram page.

Meet the Founder

Raven Wilkerson — Raven graduated from Temple University where she studied Psychology. Upon graduation she began her career working at an all-women’s halfway house facility in Camden, NJ. This is where the inspiration and motivation to develop a non-profit organization directly assisting those who are incarcerated, and their families started to develop. Following working at the halfway house, Raven worked as a child welfare case manage for 4 years. This experience emphasized the need for a program such as Out of Bounds Support. Raven would like to take all the knowledge she has gained from these experiences and provide services that will have a long lasting impact on the community.
